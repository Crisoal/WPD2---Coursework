Our Story

MentorXone was born from a joint commitment between the African Leadership College (ALC) and Glasgow Caledonian University (GCU) to empower students with the tools and guidance they need to succeed. Recognizing the importance of mentorship in shaping futures, our platform emerged as a dedicated space for connecting ambitious students with experienced mentors from diverse fields. Starting with a shared vision to bridge knowledge gaps and foster growth, we've evolved into a vibrant hub facilitating mentorship tailored for the ALC and GCU communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower ALC and GCU students with tailored mentorship programs. We provide a supportive environment where students access mentorship aligned with their goals. Through mentoring, networking, and skill-building initiatives, we equip students for success in their chosen paths.

Our Vision

We envision a community where mentorship fosters leadership and excellence. MentorXone aims to create a network of empowered leaders driving positive change across Africa and beyond. Our goal is to make mentorship a cornerstone for student success and societal impact.

The Author

Christiana Ola

As the visionary behind this platform, Christiana Ola is a final year student pursuing a B.Sc (Hons) in Computing. Passionate about leveraging technology for positive change, Christiana's dedication to empowering fellow students through mentorship led to the creation of MentorXone. Her drive to bridge knowledge gaps and foster growth within the academic community fuels her commitment to building a platform tailored for student success. Christiana envisions a future where mentorship becomes a guiding light for aspiring individuals, nurturing talent and driving societal impact.
